Real Time Vibration and Temperature Monitoring system for Predictive Maintenance

The system is designed to detect abnormal vibration and overheating in rotating machinery. When such conditions are detected, it automatically triggers alerts and performs a machine shutdown to prevent damage. The solution is intended to support predictive maintenance, and may be integrated with Machine Learning models for future pattern recognition and fault prediction.
